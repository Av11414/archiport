Garden Intellectual Space

Kampala, Uganda

Situated in Kampala, the Garden Intellectual Space is designed as a place of thought and quiet communion with the natural world. Marble and stone surfaces echo the terrain, while openings draw the garden inward — blurring the edge between built and living. The program invites contemplation, study, and a deepened awareness of one's environment.
